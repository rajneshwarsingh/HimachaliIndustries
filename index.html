Where Innovation Meets Sustainability

At Himachali Industries, we pioneer the transformation of recycled plastic sheets into premium, eco-friendly furniture—combating the urgent issue of plastic waste while championing sustainable, circular solutions. Our mission bridges functionality, style, and responsibility, offering products that serve both commercial and residential spaces with a conscience.

What we do

This is what we’re best at

Three Seater Bench

Experience outdoor seating reimagined for a sustainable world. These three-seater benches, crafted from 100% recycled plastic, combine robust durability with modern design. Weather-resistant and maintenance-free, they provide comfortable seating in parks, gardens, or public spaces, all while showcasing a commitment to environmental responsibility.

100% Recycled Plastic

Waste Bins

Redefine waste management with recycled plastic waste bins that embody sustainability and practicality. These bins are engineered for strength and longevity, with a hygienic, wipe-clean surface ideal for schools, offices, and public areas. Their sturdy build resists cracking, fading, and the elements, offering a smooth, maintenance-free experience.

100% Recycled Plastic

Flower Pots

Elevate your interiors and gardens with flower pots made entirely from recycled plastic. Designed for both beauty and resilience, these pots feature stylish, contemporary finishes and cater to a variety of plant sizes. Lightweight yet durable, they are resistant to breakage, UV light, and temperature extremes—making them perfect for both indoor and outdoor settings.

100% Recycled Plastic
